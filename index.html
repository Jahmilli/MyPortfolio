Who am I?

I am software engineering student at the University of Technology Sydney and I love to learn anything related to software development. I began learning programming in January 2016, beginning with html and CSS and have continued to dabble with it in my spare time. Through university I have primarily developed my skills in Java learning a range of software design patterns, principles and algorithms in subjects however I have found that most of this can be developed from online resources. Thus I have made it my goal to balance university subjects and learning from the web in order to maximise my development.

I am also the Treasurer of UTS Programming Society and Vice President of the 'Professional Aeronautics & Astronautics Society' in which we spark conversation and skill development into all matters related to Aerospace. This is something I am strongly passionate as I believe the utilisation of space is key to the success of humanity and its continued development. Even further, I believe that students should be provided more opportunities to learn about the industry and become more involved and ultimately I hope to assist in providing these opportunities.

I love software development as it provides an incomparible ability to create and I want to continue developing my skills with the goal of having a meaningful impact on the world.
